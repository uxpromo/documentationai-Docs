## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Registration">
    Visit `https://ui2.finzor.dev/signup`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Details">
    Enter your email and create a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Email">
    Check your inbox for a confirmation link—click to verify.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Log in with your new credentials at `https://ui2.finzor.dev/login`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">Registration may become available as development progresses.</Callout>
