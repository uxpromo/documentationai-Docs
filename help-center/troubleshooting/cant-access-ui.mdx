---
title: Can't Access Финзор UI
description: Help for users unable to open ui2.finzor.dev.
---

## Symptoms

- The Финзор UI site does not load
- Browser shows a connection error

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check URL">
    Make sure you are visiting: `https://ui2.finzor.dev`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Refresh your browser or clear cache.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another device">
    Test access on a different browser or mobile device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait and retry">
    Финзор may be updating. Try again after 10 minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
